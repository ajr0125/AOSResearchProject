A Novel Radar Detection Prototype based on Constant False Alarm Rate Concepts

Global Research Collaboration

Arjun Ramakrishnan, Minseok Shin, Yoonhyoung Park

As part of my research project, I was accepted into the AOS Global Research Program, a reserach partnership between students from the Academy of Science and the Daegu Science School in South Korea. Students work with their partners to develop a combined research project encompassing project aspects from all students involved. My South Korean research partners are Minseok Shin and Yoonhyoung Park. The individual project descriptions are shown below, and our collaboration revolves around the applications of genetic algorithms in each of our fields. Minseok's project involves applying genetic algorithms to radial net structure to improve catching nets used to prevent falling accidents on construction sites. Yoonhyoung's project involves using super enzymes to decompose microplastics in an efficient and eco-friendly manner.
