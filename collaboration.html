A Novel Radar Detection Prototype based on Constant False Alarm Rate Concepts

Global Research Collaboration

Arjun Ramakrishnan, Minseok Shin, Yoonhyoung Park

The Global Research Program is a research partnership between selected high school students from the Academy of Science and the Daegu Science School in South Korea. Students work with their partners through the year to develop and work on a combined research project encompassing project aspects from all the work of all students involved. I was selected from the Academy of Science to work with Minseok Shin and Yoonhyoung Park from Daegu Science High School. The individual project descriptions for the team are shown below, and our collaboration revolves around the applications of genetic algorithms in each of our resarch fields. Minseok's project involves applying genetic algorithms to radial net structure to improve catching nets used to prevent falling accidents on construction sites. Yoonhyoung's project involves using genetic algorithms to optimize airplane wing shapes to minimize air drag and resistance.
