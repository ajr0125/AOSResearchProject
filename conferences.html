A Novel Radar Detection Prototype based on Constant False Alarm Rate Concepts

Background - Scientist Conferences

As part of developing the foundation for this research project, I reached out to scientists who are experts in the CFAR RADAR field to clarify concepts and specifics. These conferences are linked below.

Kartik Ramakrishnan

To gain some background in the field, I talked with Kartik Ramakrishnan, Senior Systems Engineer at Ultra Electronics and expert in the area of CFAR. We primarily discussed the use of simulated data in the Radar field, as well as its possible limitations and processing needed for its use. The recording of the conversation is linked to the diamond on the left.

Russell Jeffers

In order to clarify some specifics about my project, I also talked with Dr. Russell Jeffers, another CFAR expert at Ultra Electronics. Specifically, we discussed past research around the concept of combining CFAR algorithms that my project revolves around and some potential unique directions those past projects have taken. The recording of the conversation is linked to the diamond on the left.
