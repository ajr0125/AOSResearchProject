A Novel Radar Detection Prototype based on Constant False Alarm Rate Concepts

Research on Radar Detection Algorithms

Arjun Ramakrishnan

RADAR stands for Radio Detection and Ranging. Radar technology is extremely important and can be used in a multitude of areas to save lives such as in the military for detecting enemy targets, weather forecasting for predicting dangerous weather phenomena such as tornados and biomedicine for detecting medical issues such as cell abnormalities. New and growing applications of radar systems make it critical to focus on developing and testing new ideas that could supersede present day techniques.
