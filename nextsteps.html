A Novel Radar Detection Prototype based on Constant False Alarm Rate Concepts

Next Steps

As a senior, I plan to build on this project by moving on to the next steps of radar detection: clustering and tracking. Once detections are established, they have to be grouped by similarity into actual targets through clustering. Then, their properties have to be monitored over time through tracking. This year, I am mainly focused on clustering, and I plan to apply machine learning clustering algorithms to radar detection, something that is still new. I plan to implement k-means, hierarchical clustering, and Density-Based Spatial Clustering of Applications with Noise (DBSCAN), after which I will compare their performance to the more traditional clustering algorithms in the field.
